PLM - Project 1

Professional Labor Management

Due to a scarcity of workers with specialized skill sets, company’s find it hard to access and assure quality of available talent. “Professional Labor Management” is a software tool to support in the staffing of supply chain industry material handling projects with specialized workers. PLM
incorporates a rating system to ensure quality. This software also automates common procedures within a company’s infrastructure. Accordingly, qualifying organizations may save millions in annual revenue. I created this app in collaboration with a team of senior developers. During this process, I acted as the project manager, product owner, UX/UI Designer, and QA Tester. Beta 1 was successfully launched privately and is expected to go public by the end of the year.
